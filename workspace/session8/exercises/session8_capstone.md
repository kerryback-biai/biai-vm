# Session 8: Capstone Presentation

## Your Prototype

- A working agent, skill, dashboard, or automated workflow
- Built on the AI+Code Lab (or the XYZ Corp Chatbot)
- Does not need to be production-ready — but it needs to run
- **Enterprise track:** data agent, reporting pipeline, or prediction app
- **Personal/team track:** productivity tool, workflow automation, or decision-support app
- Could extend: Session 2 dashboard, Session 4 agent, Session 7 prediction app, or something new

## Presentation Format

### Breakout Round (27 minutes)
Groups of 5-6. Each person:
- **30-second demo** — show it running, show the output
- **90-second pitch** — deployment strategy
- **1 minute feedback** from the group
- Group votes on strongest strategy for plenary

### Plenary Round (40 minutes)
5 winners present:
- **2-minute demo** — show what it does
- **3-minute pitch** — strategy, governance, Day 1 action
- **3 minutes Q&A** from the class

## Evaluation Dimensions

1. **Working Prototype** — Does it run? Does it do what you claim?
2. **Specificity** — Concrete use case, not "we'll use AI for analytics"
3. **Business Case** — Who benefits, what decision gets faster, dollar impact
4. **Data Readiness** — Where does data live? Integration challenges?
5. **Governance** — Who validates answers? What guardrails?
6. **Day 1 Action** — What happens Monday? Specific person, task, date

## Strategy Document (1-2 pages)

Include:
- Problem statement
- Proposed solution
- Deployment approach (cloud/on-prem/hybrid)
- Governance plan
- 90-day pilot with success metrics
- Day 1 action
