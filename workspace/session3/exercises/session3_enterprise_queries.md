# Session 3: Enterprise Data Queries

Platform: XYZ Corp Custom Chatbot (separate web app, not the AI Lab terminal)

## Round 1: Single-System Queries (6 minutes)

Try these — one system at a time:

**Salesforce (Industrial CRM):**
- "What is the total pipeline value for the Industrial division?"
- "Who are the top 5 customers by revenue in 2025?"

**NetSuite (Finance):**
- "What was total revenue by quarter for 2025?"
- "Show operating expenses by category for Q4"

**Workday (HR):**
- "How many employees does each division have?"
- "What is the average tenure by department?"

## Round 2: Cross-System Queries (8 minutes)

These require the chatbot to query multiple systems and merge results:

- "Compare average deal size across divisions — are some divisions closing bigger deals?"
- "What is revenue per employee by division?"
- "Compare support ticket volume to revenue by division"
- "Which division's headcount grew fastest relative to revenue?"

## Timed Challenge (5 minutes)

> Which division's top customer represents the greatest concentration risk — and what does their support ticket history look like?

This requires CRM data (customer revenue) + Zendesk (support tickets).

## Document Queries (5 minutes)

- "What is the company's policy on remote work for the Energy Division?"
- "Which vendor contracts are up for renewal in the next 6 months, and what are the minimum commitment amounts?"
- Try one of your own: ask about any policy, contract term, or procedure.

## Homework

Ask 10+ business questions in the Custom Chatbot:
- At least 3 cross-system queries
- At least 2 from the document collection
- Write down one good answer and one bad/unsatisfying answer
