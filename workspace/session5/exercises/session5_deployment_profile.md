# Session 5: Deployment and Governance Profile

## Breakout Exercise (20 minutes, groups of 3-4)

For your use case, work through these questions:

1. **Deployment:** Cloud API, on-premise, or hybrid? Why?
2. **Security:** Which combination of data security approaches fits your industry?
3. **Build vs. Buy:** Score on DUNNIXER dimensions (1-5 each):
   - Data integration
   - User experience
   - Nuance & accuracy
   - Non-functional requirements
   - Implementation timeline
   - Extensibility
   - Economics
   - Risk & governance
4. **Governance:** What controls does your organization need? Who reviews AI output?
5. **Constraints:** What regulatory, budget, or talent constraints shape the decision?

Each person takes ~4 minutes to present and get feedback.

## Individual Exercise (15 minutes)

Write your capstone deployment and governance profile:

1. **Use case** (one sentence describing your AI data agent)
2. **Deployment** (Cloud API / On-prem / Hybrid — and why)
3. **Security** (which approaches and why)
4. **Build / Buy / Extend** (recommendation with DUNNIXER-informed rationale)
5. **Governance** (what controls are needed? Who reviews output? What regulations apply?)
6. **Key risk** (single biggest risk and your mitigation plan)
